My Project Workflow: How I Handle Projects From Start to Finish

1. Discovery & Consultation +
Every project begins with understanding the client’s goals, audience, and vision. During this phase, I discuss requirements, understand business goals, identify target users, analyze competitors, and define project scope to create a strong foundation.
2. Planning & Strategy +
I create a clear project plan including system architecture, feature planning, user flow mapping, and technology selection. I recommend solutions based on scalability, performance, and budget.
3. UI/UX Design +
Before coding, I design the visual experience. This includes wireframes, UI mockups, color systems, and typography. I collaborate closely with clients to refine the design until it aligns with their vision.
4. Development Phase +
I focus on clean, maintainable code, responsive design, fast performance, and security best practices. I build everything from frontend interfaces to secure backend APIs and databases.
5. Client Collaboration & Feedback +
Communication is maintained throughout. I provide regular progress updates, preview links, and revision opportunities. This ensures clients stay involved and informed at every stage.
6. Testing & Optimization +
Before launch, the project is thoroughly tested for mobile responsiveness, browser compatibility, performance, and security issues to deliver a stable, polished, and professional product.
7. Deployment & Launch +
After final approval, I deploy the project to production. This includes domain connection, hosting setup, SSL configuration, and database deployment.
8. Post-Launch Support +
Even after deployment, I continue supporting clients with maintenance, updates, feature improvements, and bug fixes for continuous improvement.
